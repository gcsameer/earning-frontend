## üîß How to Clear Cache

If you still see third-party ads, clear your browser cache:

1. **Chrome/Edge:**
   - Press `Ctrl + Shift + Delete`
   - Select "Cached images and files"
   - Click "Clear data"

2. **Firefox:**
   - Press `Ctrl + Shift + Delete`
   - Select "Cache"
   - Click "Clear Now"

3. **Clear Service Worker:**
   - Open DevTools (F12)
   - Go to Application tab
   - Click "Service Workers"
   - Click "Unregister"

4. **Hard Refresh:**
   - Press `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac)
